## Who I am

My work sits at the intersection of strategy consulting, design, and craft. Since 2019, I've been exploring the Ethereum ecosystem, DeFi, and the governance questions that run through them.

## What this blog is about

This blog brings together notes and essays at the intersection of philosophy, finance, and forms of organization. I write here about governance, monetary design, and a few objects I am trying to understand more clearly.
