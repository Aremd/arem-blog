## AREM

Independent DeFi analysis blog.

### Principles

- **Analytical rigor**: every claim is sourced or identified as opinion
- **Independence**: no financial ties to analyzed protocols
- **Transparency**: explicit methodology, declared biases
- **Verifiability**: priority to on-chain data and primary sources

*The analyses presented on this site do not constitute financial advice.*
